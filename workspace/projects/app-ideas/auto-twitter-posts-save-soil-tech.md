# Draft Plan: Auto Twitter Posts (Save Soil + Tech)

## Idea Summary

Create an automated content scheduler for X/Twitter that posts environmental awareness (Save Soil) and technology insights.

## Problem

Consistent posting is hard without planning, scheduling, and quality controls.

## Core MVP

- Content calendar and queue
- Topic templates (Save Soil, tech news, dev tips)
- AI-assisted draft generation with manual approval
- Scheduled publishing and retry logic

## Quality Controls

- Brand voice style rules
- Duplicate post prevention
- Character limit validation
- Basic safety and policy checks

## Analytics

- Engagement tracking (likes, reposts, replies)
- Best posting time suggestions
- Top-performing topic insights

## Milestones

1. Draft generator + queue
2. Scheduler and posting engine
3. Analytics dashboard
4. Multi-account support
