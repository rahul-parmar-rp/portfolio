# Draft Plan: Automated Content Generator Platform

## Idea Summary

Create a platform that generates, edits, and schedules content across text, image, and short-form formats.

## Problem

Content teams face production bottlenecks from ideation to publishing.

## Core MVP

- Topic-to-draft generation pipeline
- Multi-format output templates
- Human approval workflow
- Scheduled publishing queue

## Quality Controls

- Brand style guides
- Fact-check checkpoints
- Plagiarism screening
- Audience intent alignment

## Milestones

1. Draft generator
2. Workflow approvals
3. Multi-channel scheduler
4. Analytics and optimization
