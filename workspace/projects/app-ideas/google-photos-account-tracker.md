# Draft Plan: Google Photos Account Activity Tracker

## Idea Summary

Build a tracker for multiple Google accounts to monitor status, usage patterns, and maintenance tasks.

## Problem

Managing many accounts manually causes missed checks and inconsistent maintenance.

## Core MVP

- Account inventory and health status
- Scheduled check-in reminders
- Notes and risk flags per account
- Activity timeline dashboard

## Compliance Notes

- Use only official authentication and APIs
- Respect provider terms and limits
- Keep account owner consent explicit

## Milestones

1. Account registry
2. Reminder and status engine
3. Dashboard and exports
4. Team sharing permissions
